* Method OVerloading
Overloading refers to making a method perform different operations based on the nature of its arguments.
```
def demo(self, a, b, c, d=5, e=None):
        print("a =", a)
        print("b =", b)
        print("c =", c)
        print("d =", d)
        print("e =", e)
```
